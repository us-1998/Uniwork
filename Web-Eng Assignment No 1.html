The James Webb Space Telescope



The James Webb Space Telescope, launched in 2021, is the most powerful space telescope ever built. It allows us to peer further into the cosmos than ever before, potentially unlocking secrets about the early universe and distant exoplanets. This revolutionary instrument is set to transform our understanding of astronomy and our place in the vast expanse of space.
